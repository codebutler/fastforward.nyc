Fast Forward: The Plan to Modernize New York City Transit

On May 23rd, the MTA and Andy Byford revelaed #FastForwardNYC — their plan to modernize New York City’s subways and buses.

It’s excellent: state-of-the-art subway signals in 10 years instead of 40, enhanced station experience, a total bus network redesign in all five boroughs, vastly accelerated accessibility upgrades, and revamped customer service, among many other improvements.

We aren’t affiliated with the MTA, but we happened to have this domain and thought we could use it to help pressure our elected officials to make Fast Forward happen.
